Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is a popular Mexican dip made from mashed avocados mixed with lime juice, salt, and additional ingredients such as tomatoes, onions, cilantro, and jalape√±os. Known for its creamy texture and fresh, zesty flavor, guacamole is often served with tortilla chips, in tacos, or as a topping for various Mexican dishes.


Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt.
  2. Mix in the diced onion, chopped cilantro, diced tomatoes, and minced garlic.
  3. Stir in cayenne pepper, if desired.
  4. Refrigerate for 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
