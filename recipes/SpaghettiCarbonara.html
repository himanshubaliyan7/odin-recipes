Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. Simple yet incredibly delicious!

Ingredients

  • 200 grams spaghetti
  • 100 grams pancetta, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50 grams Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 50 grams Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente.
  2. In a separate pan, cook the pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, Pecorino Romano, and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain the spaghetti, reserving some of the pasta water.
  5. Quickly toss the hot pasta with the pancetta and garlic, then remove from heat.
  6. Add the egg and cheese mixture, tossing quickly to ensure the eggs do not scramble. Add a little reserved pasta water if the sauce is too thick.
  7. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  8. Serve immediately, with extra grated cheese on top if desired.
